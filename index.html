Welcome to my website! I am an MEng student at MIT studying Artificial Intelligence and Decision Making (Course 6-4). I am in the Cyborg Pyschology group under Prof. Pat Pataranutaporn in the Media Lab.

My primary research interests lie in mechanistic interpretability for both scientific understanding of LLMs and improving their safety. I also am interested in how we can apply mechanistic interpretability to create interfaces that allow us to better monitor and control AI, for both technical and non-technical users.

Outside of work, I enjoy traveling, and learning other languages, hiking, videogames, and everything science fiction.
