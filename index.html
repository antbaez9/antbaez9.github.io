Welcome to my website! I am currently an MEng student at MIT studying Artificial Intelligence and Decision Making (Course 6-4). I currently work in the Media Lab in the Cyborg Pyschology group with Prof. Pat Pataranutaporn on applying insights from mechanistic interpretability for human-computer interfaces.

I previously worked in Luca Daniel's group on improving Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs) by guaranteeing conservation laws. Last summer, I worked at DeepAI on improving text-to-video models. You can find out more about my past and ongoing research and projects on the tabs above.

Outside of work, I am a member of Phi Delta Theta. I also enjoy traveling and learning other languages, hiking, playing videogames, and science fiction books and movies.
